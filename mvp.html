POSTING ADVERTISMENTS ON THE MVP PLATFORM

Step by Step guide:

Login to Doubleclick for Publishers - “DFP”

You have 2 scenarios here, you want to add an advertisment for an Anthro MVP athlete or create a 3rd Party Ad that we receive payment from. I will explain both methods below:

MVP WAY

  1. After logged in to DFP, click on Inventory and CREATE a new Ad Unit
  2. Include the appropriate fields for:
    • Name with size, for example Richard_Sherman_FathersDay_300x250. Use underscores becauses spaces are not allowed.
    • Enter the proposed Ad Size.
    • Add it to the specific placement. These will be setup for you prior to adding the ad.
  3. Next go into the Outstanding order on the “Orders” page for that athlete. We have 2 types of orders, TILES & BANNERS. Enter the one of your choosing.
  4. Click new order
  5. Enter the name, (same way as Ad Unit), Advertiser(House), Labels (Athlete’s last name), Setting Type: Sponsorship. Select the placement as well.
  6. Add a Creative, upload image, and enter the creative click-through destination
  7. Make Sure the Creative is Active/Approved/Delivering.
  8. Pause any unwanted Ads
  9. MAKE SURE AD IS NOT DISPLAYING ON OTHER SITES

3RD PARTY

  1. After logged in to DFP, click on Inventory and CREATE a new Ad Unit
  2. Include the appropriate fields for:
    • Name with size, for example Richard_Sherman_FathersDay_300x250. Use underscores becauses spaces are not allowed.
    • Enter the proposed Ad Size.
    • Add it to the specific placement. These will be setup for you prior to adding the ad.
  3. Create a new Order
  4. Enter the name, (same way as Ad Unit), Advertiser(House), Labels (Athlete’s last name), Setting Type: Sponsorship. Select the placement as well.
  5. Add a Creative, upload image, and enter the creative click-through destination
  6. Go into Inventory
  7. Click Generate tags on the sidebar
  8. Choose the AD UNITS to put on page.
  9. Send tags to Developers for installation on the site.
  10. MAKE SURE AD IS NOT DISPLAYING ON OTHER SITES
